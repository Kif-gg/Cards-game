Privacy policy

Last Updated: [Date]

Spades Madness is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our web app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

Username: To identify you within the app.

Email Address: For account creation, communication, and password recovery.

Password: To secure your account. Passwords are securely hashed and not stored in plain text.

Cookies: We use cookies to store your preferences, such as dark mode settings.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

Account Creation: To enable you to register and use the app.

User Preferences: To personalize your experience (e.g., saving dark mode settings).

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

4. User Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

Change Password: You can update your password at any time through your account settings.

Delete Account: You can request account deletion, which will permanently remove your data from our systems.

Contact Us: You can contact us via the feedback page or through the contact information provided in the app's footer.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for one year. If you do not log in for a year, your account and data will be automatically deleted. You may request deletion earlier by contacting us.

6. Security Measures

We employ the following security measures to protect your data:

Password Hashing: Passwords are stored using secure algorithms.

Brute Force Protection: We limit login attempts and temporarily lock accounts after multiple failed attempts.

7. Children's Privacy

Spades Madness is designed to be all-age friendly. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from users under a certain age. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via email or through notices on our web app.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us from the feedback page or via email.
